2024 IN-PERSON Meetings at Parky's Farm

  • September 14, 2024 @ Noon

The location is Parky's Farm, 10073 Daly Rd, Cincinnati, OH 45231

Link to location

The meeting begins at NOON with a potluck luncheon (Please bring a dish to share), followed by the presentation at 12:30 pm. Weather permitting, we will have an open hive demonstration at approximately 1:30 pm. Please bring whatever protective clothing you prefer. Although the meeting officially begins at NOON, we will be setting up at 11:30 so feel free to come earlier to talk bees. You know we all like to talk bees!!

SWOBA will provide a meat/cheese tray, bread, condiments, and drinks.

Again, please bring a food dish to share
