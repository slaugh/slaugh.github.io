About SWOBA Membership

You can register to become a member of the SouthWestern Ohio Beekeepers Association by clicking the SWOBA MEMBERSHIP FORM button below. It only costs $5 yearly to join and this fee allows us to organize our in-person meetups, pay for a Zoom license for our online meetings, and continue to run this website.
